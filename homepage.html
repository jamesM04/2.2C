Workout Tracker

Homepage

The workout tracker website is designed to assist fitness enthusiasts in planning and tracking their workouts and ensures the application of progressive overload within the trainees exercise regimen. Progressive overload is the idea that the development of muscle mass and strength is promoted through an increase in reps and weight over time. Various other features are included in this website to assist the trainee in their fitness journey as outline below:

Sections

1. Homepage – Welcoming page to introduce the website features to the user and allow them to navigate to other sections.

2. Routine Creator – Users can make their own routines that allows users to add exercises, and prescribe reps and sets, enabling users to organise their workouts.

3. Workout Tracker – Interface the allows users to choose from a routine they have created and then track sets, reps, and weight of the listed exercises.

4. Calorie Calculator - Calculator that provides users with their daily energy expenditure, which can be used to find the calories required to lose or gain weight.
